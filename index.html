Academic Design Projects

[ IoT - AUTONOMOUS RV]

Self-driving cars are becoming a reality. As with any emergent technology, issues arise as they are introduced into society. This design utilizes an Internet of Things interface to address user pain points of trust and safety in autonomous RV’s.

My Role: Sketching, Ideation, Interaction Design, Usability Testing
[INCLUSIVE DESIGN - VOLUNTEER BRIDGE ]

The CHI 2016 design brief Leveling The Playing Field challenges us to look closely at populations left behind in the advancement of technology. An ever-growing population fits under this category: isolated elders. Volunteer Bridge addresses the desires of isolated elders with an age-sensitive interface.

My Role: User Research, Personas, Ideation, Storytelling, Strategy
[ CONVERSATIONAL UI - CHEF ]

Yelp users find searching for a restaurant time consuming and laborious. Chef leverages artificial intelligence and a refined content structure for a more streamlined search experience.

My Role: Facilitator, User Research, Personas, Wireframing, Usability Testing
