I am a first-year graduate student at Indiana University Bloomington pursuing my M.S. in Human-Computer Interaction design. Prior to beginning my masters, I worked on producing web content and developing digital marketing strategy for a variety of clients including large corporations, non-profits, start-ups, and small businesses.

My passion for curating digital experiences led me to pursue my master’s in HCI/d. I believe design is about creating an experience that is seamless, functional, and joyful to the user. I enjoy getting lost in the messiness of the whole design process and put a strong emphasis on collaboration and listening.

More about me: I was born and raised in Northern California. I love to explore the world whether it’s through books, traveling, movies, or art. When I’m not designing, you can find me running, listening to music, or trying new foods.
